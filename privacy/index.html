Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GetMyCleaningQuote ("we", "us") collects and uses your information when you use our website and quote-matching service.

Information we collect

When you request a quote, we collect the information you provide: your name, email address, phone number, ZIP code, and details about the cleaning service you need. We also automatically collect standard analytics data such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

How we use it

We use your information to match you with local cleaning professionals, to operate and improve our service, and to communicate with you about your request. We share the job details you submit with relevant cleaning providers so they can offer you a quote.

Analytics & cookies

We use Google Tag Manager and Vercel Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. See our Cookie Policy for details.

Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by emailing support@getmycleaningquote.com.
