About GetMyCleaningQuote

We make finding a great local cleaner simple, fast, and free.

GetMyCleaningQuote is a free matching service that connects homeowners and businesses with vetted, highly-rated cleaning professionals in their area. Instead of calling around for quotes, you tell us what you need once and we match you with local pros who compete for your business.

What we do

We partner with background-checked, insured cleaning companies across more than 100 US metro areas. When you request a quote, we share your job details with available local pros so you can compare prices, read reviews, and book with confidence — all at no cost to you.

Why homeowners use us

Comparing quotes is the easiest way to get a fair price. Our service is 100% free, carries no obligation, and takes under two minutes. You stay in control of who you hire.
